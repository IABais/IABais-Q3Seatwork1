Bleach Thousand Year Blood War Arc

Synopsis:

Bleach Thousand-Year Blood War is based on the final story arc of the manga, pitching Ichigo Kurosaki, the Soul Society, and his allies, friends against a new enemy known as the Wandenreich. The Wandenreich, a group within the Quincy, will finally rise to strike back against the Shinigami and the Soul Society, resulting in an all-out war between the two factions.

Characters:

Ichigo Kurosaki

Is a human who is also a substitute shinigami. He is the son of Isshin and Masaki Kurosaki. In this arc Soul society is attacked by Quincies a race that was supposedly wiped in the past. It is up to him to save Soul society and his freinds. His voice actor is Masakazu Morita for the Japanese voice and Johnny Yong Bosch for the English voice.

Yhwach

Yhwach is the king and father of the Quinces and leads the attack on Soul society. He is the Emperor of Wandenriech. And he is the son of Soul King. His voice actor is Takayuki Sugo for the Japanese voice and Richard Epcar for the English voice.

Sosuke Aizen

He was a memeber of the Gotei 13 or a captain in Soul Society. But he later on betreyed them and formed the Espada. He was defeated by Ichigo Kurosaki and was locked in jail underneath Soul Society headquarters. His Voice actor is Sho Hayami for the Japanese voice and Kyle Hebert for the English voice.

Urahara Kisuke

He was the former captain of the 12th division and was the founder and 1st President of the S.R.D.I(Shinigami Research and Developent Institute). He now lives in the Human world and owns a small shop that sells shinigami items. His English voice is Doug Erholtz and his Japanese voice actor is Shinichiro Miki.
